Welcome to my blog.

My name is Jorge Robles and I am a professionally licensed K-12 Music Teacher in NYS, specializing in instrumental music such as band as well as a Full-Stack Software Engineer.

Today is February 24th, 2022 and I have been learning HTML and CSS for about five weeks, with this fifth week being my first foray into JavaScript. This is my starting point for this blog. This blog will detail the slow, grueling process of learning programming, and specifically full-stack web developement from scratch. I have never actually coded or programmed before and have spent the past six years of my life so far training to become a music teacher before the COVID-19 pandemic damaged my profession.

This blog is mainly for me to express my thoughts about programming, transitioning from music, and pretty much whatever is on my mind. If you are reading this, that means I have somehow made it into the industry and hopefully this blog inspires you to take the leap of faith yourself.

The current version of this website is written using HTML5 and vanilla CSS. No frameworks, just plain and simple CSS. This website will be pushed to GitHub where you will hopefully see the website's progression into it's final form by the end of my self-taught journey.

Strap on, and click here to begin reading my journey.
